Refund & Return Policy

📦 Return Eligibility

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if:

  • The item is damaged or defective.
  • You received the wrong product.
  • The item is unused and in its original packaging.

💳 Refund Conditions

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

❌ Non-Returnable Items

  • Used or damaged products not due to our error
  • Items without original packaging
  • Limited edition or final sale items

📝 Return Process

  1. Email us at returns@multibazaar.in with your order ID and reason.
  2. We will confirm the return and share shipping instructions.
  3. Send the product back using any local courier (with tracking).

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Refunds are not guaranteed for items returned without approval.
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the error is on our side.
