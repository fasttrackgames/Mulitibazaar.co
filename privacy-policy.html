Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 28, 2025

1. Introduction

At Multibazar, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website or make purchases from our store.

2. Information We Collect

  • Your name, phone number, email, and shipping address
  • Order and payment details
  • Technical data such as device type, browser, and IP address
  • Cookies and usage data for improving user experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and deliver products
  • To communicate about orders, refunds, or issues
  • To improve our products, website, and services
  • To send offers, updates, and newsletters (optional)

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only with:

  • Trusted delivery partners to fulfill your order
  • Payment gateways like Razorpay for secure transactions
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use modern security protocols like SSL, firewalls, and encrypted payment channels to protect your information. Payment information is handled only by trusted partners like Razorpay.

6. Cookies

Cookies help us personalize your shopping experience and improve our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
